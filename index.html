About GreenBite Microgreens

What are Microgreens?

Microgreens are young vegetable greens, approximately 1-3 inches tall. They are harvested after the cotyledon leaves have developed (and possibly, with one set of true leaves). They are bursting with flavor, often more intense than mature greens, and come in a variety of colors and textures, making them a delightful addition to any meal.

Packed with Health Benefits

Despite their small size, microgreens are packed with nutrients. Studies show they can contain significantly higher levels of vitamins (like C, E, and K) and carotenoids than their mature counterparts. They are a fantastic way to boost your intake of antioxidants and beneficial plant compounds, supporting overall health and well-being.

Sustainably Grown by GreenBite

At GreenBite, we are committed to sustainable and eco-friendly farming. We grow our microgreens locally, using organic, non-GMO seeds and a soil-less hydroponic system that conserves water. Our greens are grown without pesticides or harmful chemicals, ensuring you receive the freshest, purest, and most nutritious product, straight from our urban farm to your table.
